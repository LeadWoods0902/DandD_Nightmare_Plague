## World State
### Quinn
Gifted a scale by Kaede. taken prisoner by slavers and transported by ship to an unknown country. Taught common by the slavers during the 2 year Journey. During the time on the ship, a neutral passenger who remained hooded at all times would often sneak into the hold to feed and talk to you - given his language, you assume he is from Chimo like you. Near the end of it's journey, the ship was ambushed by an unknown force, which seemingly killed most of the crew. Other than seeing the inky smoke envelope the captain, you have only the word of the remaining crew (Herouk, Jareth & Seraphina) to go on. Given the dire situation, Herouk forced the other two and some of the slaves into repairing the ship
